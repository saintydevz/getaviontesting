    <h1>Actors</h1>
    <h2>getactors()</h2>
    <p>Returns a list of actors you can execute in.</p>
    
    <div class="function-signature">
        <code>function getactors(): table&lt;number, Actor&gt;</code>
    </div>
    
    <div class="example">
        <p>Loop through all actors in the list and print them into the console.</p>
        <pre><code class="language-lua">
for index, actor in getactors() do 
    print(index, actor)
end
        </code><button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyCode(this)">Copy</button></pre>
    </div>

    <h2>run_on_actor()</h2>
    <p>Runs a script on an actor thread. You cannot pass types like tables or functions as they are from a different Lua VM.</p>
    
    <div class="function-signature">
        <code><nil> run_on_actor(&lt;Actor&gt; actor, &lt;string&gt; script, ...)</code>
    </div>
    
    <div class="example">
        <p>Retrieve the first actor and print it into the console.</p>
        <pre><code class="language-lua">
run_on_actor(getactors()[1], [[
    print("Hello, world!")
]])
        </code><button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyCode(this)">Copy</button></pre>
    </div>

    <h2>get_comm_channel()</h2>
    <div class="function-signature">
        <code>&lt;BindableEvent&gt; get_comm_channel(&lt;number&gt; id)</code>
    </div>
    <p>Returns an existing communication channel.</p>

       <h2>create_comm_channel()</h2>
    <div class="function-signature">
        <code>function create_comm_channel(): (number, BindableEvent)</code>
    </div>
    <p>Returns a channel and ID for data transfer between the actor state and your state.</p>

       <h2>run_on_thread()</h2>
    <div class="function-signature">
        <code>function run_on_thread(actor_thread: thread, string: string, (opt) channel_data: any) : ()</code>
    </div>
    <p>Runs a script on the first available actor thread (only works if actors are present).</p>
        <div class="example">
        <p>Retrieve the first actor and print it into the console.</p>
        <pre><code class="language-lua">
run_on_thread(getactorthreads()[1], [[
    print("Hello, world!")
]])
        </code><button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyCode(this)">Copy</button></pre>
    </div>

    print(index, thread)

        </code><button class="copy-btn" onclick="copyCode(this)">Copy</button></pre>

    <ul>

    <div class="example">
